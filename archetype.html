What's an Archetype?

An archetype is a standard pattern of the behavior of a character. Some would call it a stereotype, while others refer to it as an ideal template. Archetypes help determine a character’s role and drive throughout the story. You may already know of the Hero, the Wizard, and Princess, but they are many more. So many that it might take this entire page. Fortunately, Psychiatrist Carl Jung figured out a way to simplify them by grouping them based on common traits, such as the archetype’s motivations, desires, and goals. As a result, there are 12 main archetypes.

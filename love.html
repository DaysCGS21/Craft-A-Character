Lover

"You're the only one"

The Lover is a passionate seeker on the quest for love. While romantic love is the most common, they can also seek familial love and friendship. With the drive of intimacy and relationship, they show devotion and passion for the one they love. As they fear being alone or unloved, they tend to desire to please others, at the risk of losing their own identity. Despite this, the Lover can motivate the audience to seek love and appreciate the ones they have.

Also known as:

  • Partner
  • Friend
  • Intimate
  • Enthusiast
  • Sensualist
  • Spouse

Qualities

  • Devotion
  • Selflessness
  • Passion

Flaws

  • Self-Sacrifice for Another
  • Emotionally Vulnerable

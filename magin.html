Magician

"To make things happen"

The Magician archetype represents the carrier and user of knowledge in magic, science, and/or technology. They are highly intellectual visionaries who seek to understand the fundamental laws of the universe. They develop a vision and live by it by finding win-win solutions. Despite this, they tend to fear unintended negative consequences. Magicians encourage the audience to seek the truth.

Also known as:

  • Visionary
  • Inventor
  • Shaman
  • Mage
  • Tech guy/girl

Qualities

  • Knowledge
  • Strategic
  • Experience
  • Experimental

Flaws

  • Hurbis
  • Indignant
  • Willingness to Destroy

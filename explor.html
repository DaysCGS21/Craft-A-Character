Explorer

"Life is to explore"

The explorer is the traveler always on a journey. They like to experience new things and stay off the grid. Being resourceful and cultured, they tend to be ahead of their time. Their core desire is to experience a more fulfilling life. As a result, they fear conformity and settling in one spot, which could result in aimless wandering and forgetting about the people and things they also value. Fortunately, the Explorer pushes the audience to seek new things and experiences.

Also known as:

  • Wanderer
  • Seeker
  • Pioneer
  • Pilgrim
  • Adventurer

Qualities

  • Resourceful
  • Adapting
  • Curiousity
  • Innovative

Flaws

  • Overly Independent
  • Easily Distracted
  • Distracted from Self

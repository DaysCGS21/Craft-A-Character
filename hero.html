Hero

"If there's a will, there's a way"

The Hero represents overcoming obstacles to achieve a goal. They are meant to inspire the audience to face everyday problems. Their goal is to master something to improve the world, something that depends on the story. It could be strength, a power, an object, and even a quality. They fear vulnerability and not being able to act. Hero's are one of the most common archetypes seen in protagonists.

Also known as:

  • Warrior
  • Crusador
  • Superhero
  • Soldier
  • Rescuer

Qualities

  • Courage
  • Confidence
  • Strength

Flaws

  • Ignorance
  • Arrogance
  • Egotism
  • Overconfidence

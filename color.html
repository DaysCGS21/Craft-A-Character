Pick your Theme

For media that includes color, Color is a major tool for design. It conveys the message or theme the creator is trying to portray. Such example is when green is used to represent flora and nature, while blue and pink colors are used or gender reveals in baby showers. For characters, it lets us know about them, their personality, lifestyle, and even standards. It isn’t always the case, but it is a common design tactic. Like many Pokemon, Pikachu’s color represents its type, yellow for electricity, while Mario’s colors represent his courage and energy. Of course, colors can also be used as a subversion of the trope, allowing the creator to provide a message. Luke Skywalker starts out with pale colors as he starts on the light side of the force. Through the movies, his colors start to darken as his connection to Darth Vader becomes deeper. In Return of Jedi, he wears black attire as he is close to falling to the dark side. However, he chooses not to, becoming a true jedi. As this example shows, colors can also be used to tell a story within the character.

Red

Red is the color of power, strength, energy, and passion. When we see it, it can raise our blood pressure. It is a very attractive color, resulting in it being used to signify danger. However, it can also signify love. Red tends to be used to symbolize fire when it comes to elements. It can also signify anger, war, and wrath. Characters who have red tend to be leaders, passionate individuals, and/or courageous folk.

Orange

Orange is the color of energy, determination, enthusiasm, and excitement. It combines the energy of red and the happiness of yellow. It catches the eyes’ attention and can give a cheerful feeling. Orange can symbolize Might or physical attacks. The characters in orange are usually optimistic and energetic, at times physically active. Although, it can also stand for arrogance.

Yellow

Yellow is the color of joy and brightness. It is the most attractive color to the eye, which can result as disturbing. Yellow tends to represent lightning and electricity. Characters in yellow are often positive and optimistic, spreading their energy towards others. It also tends to represent gold, which is used on characters in a high position. However, yellow can also stand for cowardice.

Green

Green is the color of nature, growth, harmony, freshness and fertility. It is the color most associated with flora. It combines the calmness of blue and the joy of yellow. For human metabolism, it can calm the mind and produce tranquility. It is also highly associated with health and vitality, as it is often used in HP bars and nutrition guides. When it comes to elements, it is mostly used for flora powers, but can be used on wind and earth. Characters in green tend to be caregivers, explorers, innocents, or are more affiliated with nature. It can also signify luck. Though, it can also stand for envy.

Blue

Blue is the color of calmness, peace, sympathy, idealism, nobility, and spirituality. It is one of the colors that helps calm the human metabolism. However, it can also represent sadness. Blue is often used to symbolize water, but it can also represent ice. Characters in blue are usually noble, calm and collective, and wise. It is also the color that represents the male.

Purple

Purple/Violet is the color of nobility, luxury, elegance, mystery, wisdom and maturity. It combines the energy of red and the calmness of blue. Due to its qualities, purple is associated with royalty. With elements, purple is the most associated with magic and mind powers. Characters in purple tend to be elegant, magicall, mysterious, mature, and creative. However, it can also stand for mischief, pride, and dark powers.

Pink

Pink is the color of love, vulnerability, youth, innocence, sweetness, and hope. It combines the energy of red and the purity of white. It can be an immature color, often seen in youthful individuals. However, it can represent naivete, inexperience, and weakness. Pink can represent unusual elements, such as love, good magic, enchantments, and at times wind. Characters in pink can be upbeat, passionate, and innocent. It is also the color that represents femininity.

Brown

Brown is the color of normality, honesty, maturity, reliability, and oldness. It is a common color seen throughout the world. It has the negative traits of dullness and being predictable. The element mostly associated with this color is earth. While it is a common color in characters, those in mostly brown can be generic, dependable, or warming.

Black

Black is the color of power, elegance, formality, mystery, and darkness. Many associate it with death, lack of emotions, evil, and malice. However, it can also represent rebelliousness and maturity. It is also quite commonly used as it contrasts with light aspects. The obvious use of Black in elements is for dark powers and shadow magic. While it is commonly used for villain characters, it can also be used for rebellious characters, as well as experienced individuals and those who try to not be noticed.

White

White is the color of light, goodness, innocence, purity, virginity, and cleaness. Being the opposite of black, it is the color most associated with divinity (besides gold). However, it can also signify lack of emotion, intense order, or nothingness. As an element, it represents light powers. Characters in white are usually the innocent, the pure, the saint, or the righteous. Though, they can also be strict.

Creator

"If you can imagine it, it can be done"

The Creator uses their creativity to create manifestations. They are imaginative and visionary, aiming to create valuable things that will be remembered. As they express their own vision, they fear mediocre visions and execution, causing them to become perfectionists. Regardless, they inspire the audience to be creative and move with their ideas.

Also known as:

  • Artist
  • Inventor
  • Innovator
  • Musician
  • Writer
  • Dreamer

Qualities

  • Drive
  • Creativity
  • Passion
  • Vision

Flaws

  • Perfectionism
  • Egotism
  • Self-Sacrifice

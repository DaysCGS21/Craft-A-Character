Ruler

"Power isn't everything, it's the only thing"

The ruler is one who takes control to stop the chaos. They hold the ability to lead with high status and prestige and are the ones to make the decisions. Using power, they aim to create prosperity in what or whom they rule over. Fearing chaos or being overthrown, they run the risk of becoming authoritarian and ignoring criticism. One major thing they teach the audience is to take responsibility.

Also known as:

  • Leader
  • Boss
  • King/Queen
  • Emperor
  • Sovereign

Qualities

  • Leadership
  • Power
  • Responsibility
  • Charisma

Flaws

  • Suspicious
  • Paranoia
  • Refusing Help
  • Prone to be Corrupted

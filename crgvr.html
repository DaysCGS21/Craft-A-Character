Caregiver

"Love your neighbors as you love yourself"

The Caregiver is the one who takes care of others. They are protective and passionate, driving them to provide assistance. As this generosity can be exploited, they fear selfishness and ingratitude. With that said, they help the audience learn to be generous and compassionate.

Also known as:

  • Saint
  • Altruist
  • Parent
  • Helper
  • Supporter
  • Medic
  • Cleric

Qualities

  • Loving Nature
  • Selflessness
  • Generousity

Flaws

  • Naivete
  • Exploitable
